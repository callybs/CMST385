Restored "Let It Be" Film on Disney+

Date: May 8, 2024

The original 1970 film "Let It Be," directed by Michael Lindsay-Hogg, has been meticulously restored by Peter Jackson's team and is now available on Disney+ as of May 8, 2024. This marks the first time the film has been available in over 50 years. The restoration follows the success of Jackson's docuseries "The Beatles: Get Back" and includes footage not seen in the series. [Read more]

John Lennon's "Mind Games Ultimate Collection"

Date: June 2024

An Ultimate Collection box set of John Lennon's 1973 album "Mind Games" is set for release in June 2024. This collection follows the format of previous reissues for "Imagine" and "Plastic Ono Band," and it will include six albums' worth of material along with an accompanying hardback book. [Read more]

Final Performances of "The Beatles LOVE" by Cirque du Soleil

Date: July 7, 2024

The long-running Cirque du Soleil show "The Beatles LOVE," which has been a staple at The Mirage in Las Vegas since 2006, will have its final performance on July 7, 2024. This announcement comes as The Mirage begins its transformation into the Hard Rock Las Vegas. The show has been celebrated for its innovative integration of The Beatles' music with Cirque du Soleil's acrobatics and choreography. [Read more]
